About AlexStocks

Real-time market sentiment analytics platform

What is AlexStocks?

AlexStocks helps you understand what people are talking about in the stock market. We track conversations across multiple financial discussion communities and show you the trending stocks, what sentiment surrounds them, and how the buzz is changing over time.

What You'll Find Here

  • Trending Stocks: See which tickers are being discussed the most in the last 24 hours
  • Sentiment Analysis: Understand if the overall mood is positive, negative, or neutral for any stock
  • Live Stock Prices: Current market prices updated throughout the day
  • Discussion Volume: Track how much buzz and attention different stocks are getting
  • Historical Trends: See how sentiment and discussion volume change over time
  • Interactive Charts: Visualize hourly mentions and daily sentiment patterns

How It Works

We continuously monitor financial discussion forums and social communities where investors share their thoughts and opinions. The platform automatically identifies which stocks are being mentioned, analyzes whether the sentiment is positive or negative, and presents this information in an easy-to-understand format.

Our Data Sources

We gather discussions from multiple major financial communities, including popular forums focused on retail trading, general market discussions, and long-term investing strategies. Stock price information comes from reliable market data providers to give you accurate, real-time pricing alongside the sentiment data.
